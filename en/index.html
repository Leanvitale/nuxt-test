Your Own Investment Fund

Managing your own investment fund can bring many benefits in the medium and long term, and can be an excellent strategy for an experienced investor or trader looking to climb the ranks in the financial industry. However, it's important to note that managing an investment fund comes with several challenges:

Doing the math
For precise investor stake calculations, avoid rounding errors to prevent long-term negative consequences and losses for the fund.
Support time
Investors will want to know how their investments are evolving, which will often require valuable time from the fund manager to verify basic data.
Focus
Maintaining focus is crucial when managing a fund. Administering the fund can be stressful and complicated, diverting effort from financial operations.
